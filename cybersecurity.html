Cybersecurity: Protecting Your Digital World

Why Cybersecurity Matters

Cybersecurity protects individuals and businesses from cyber threats such as data breaches, hacking, and identity theft.

Common Cyber Threats

  • Phishing: Fraudulent emails trick users into providing sensitive information.
  • Malware: Viruses and ransomware can damage systems.
  • Social Engineering: Hackers manipulate people into revealing information.

How to Stay Safe Online

Use strong passwords, enable two-factor authentication, and avoid suspicious links to protect yourself online.

Conclusion

Cybersecurity is essential for everyone in a digital world. Stay vigilant and secure your data.
